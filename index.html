MoreCast

Weather Tracker

Morecast is a powerful and user-friendly weather application that provides accurate and detailed weather information to users. With its sleek and intuitive interface, Morecast offers a seamless experience for anyone seeking reliable weather forecasts. The app combines data from multiple sources, including global weather models and local weather stations, to deliver precise and up-to-date weather predictions.

Users can access a wide range of features, such as hourly and 14-day forecasts, radar maps, satellite imagery, and severe weather alerts. Morecast also provides additional information like temperature, precipitation, wind speed, and humidity, allowing users to plan their activities accordingly and stay prepared for any weather conditions.

Living Atlas

Map of the World

The Living Atlas of the World is a comprehensive and dynamic collection of geographic data and maps provided by Esri, a leading provider of geographic information system (GIS) technology. It serves as a valuable resource for exploring and understanding our planet's ever-changing landscapes, ecosystems, and cultural features. The Living Atlas offers a vast array of curated maps, imagery, and data layers sourced from reputable organizations and individuals worldwide.

These resources cover a wide range of themes, including demographics, climate, terrain, biodiversity, and more. With its constantly updated content and interactive tools, the Living Atlas provides researchers, educators, and decision-makers with the necessary information to study, analyze, and make informed decisions about our complex world. It acts as a gateway to exploring and visualizing spatial data, fostering a deeper understanding of our global environment.
